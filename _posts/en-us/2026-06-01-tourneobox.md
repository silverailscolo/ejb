I designed this handy camping kit for the Ford Grand Tourneo Connect/Volkswagen Caddy Maxi 7-seater.

The TourneoBox fits exactly behind the (folded) 2nd row seats and uses the full interior width of 119 cm.
The bed is 119 cm wide by 180 cm long. If you want a longer bed, order the TourneoBoxLong version measuring 119x200 cm.

By keeping the top flush with the folded second row seats, you can sit upright on top without the need for a pop-up roof.

At the rear, the TourneoBox contains two 40 x 60 cm lockable drawers, compartmented to your choice.
Two compact suitcases fit under the drawers on the left and right, supporting for a tidy journey.

Store long objects and a compact jerry in the center bay.

At the front right, the base contains a recess for the [Boxio Toilet](https://boxio.nl/products/urinedivergerend-toilet-starterset) (not included). By flipping the right seat forward and taking off the hatch, you can use it any time.

<h2>Bonus tip: Turn off SAFE LOCK when sleeping in your van</h2>

<p>We had to discover this for ourselves. The campsite neighbors must have noticed...</p>

<p>To lock your van when you sleep inside, you have to press <i class="fa-solid fa-lock"></i> on the VW/Ford key fob <b>twice</b> (within 2 seconds).
This turns off the interior sensors, and - whenever required - you can use any door handle to exit.</p>

<p>With SAFE LOCK (a single press), the interior door handles are inactive and the alarm will sound as soon as you move!
But still, the alarm will go off when you open a door anyway, so a single press on <i class="fa-solid fa-unlock"></i> on the key fob before touching a latch is a more elegant way to get out at night.</p>

<p>Good night!</p>
