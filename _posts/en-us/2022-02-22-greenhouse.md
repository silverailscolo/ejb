In 2022, in collaboration with a professional greenhouse contractor, we built a bespoke greenhouse annex to our garden shed. Only for the foundation we had to use pristine cement, cast in a wooden formwork made from reused scaffolding boards. General dimensions WxLxH: 4m x 4m x 4m
