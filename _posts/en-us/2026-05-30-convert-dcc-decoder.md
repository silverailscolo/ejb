## How to add a DCC decoder to an N-scale Arnold SW-1500 Calf

Converting N-scale DC engines to DCC allows you to run them on your DCC layout.
In this post, I'll show how I added a TCS M4 decoder to my [Arnold N engine](/rollingstock/UP_SW1500_1870/).

## What we need

- A [TCS Z2 4-function DCC decoder](https://www.tcsdcc.com/product-page/z2) in a small form factor.
- Kapton insulating tape
- Soldering iron and -wire

- Remove the shell from the frame
